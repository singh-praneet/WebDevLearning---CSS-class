ABOUT LTS

Welcome to My Little Taco Shop

At Taco Shap, we believe that great food brings people together, and there's nothing quite like the joy of indulging in delicious, mouth-watering tacos. Our passion for authentic Mexican cuisine led us to create a haven for taco lovers, where the flavors of Mexico come to life.

Founded in JULY 2023 by "Vivek Gupta" , Taco Shop is a family-owned and operated restaurant committed to serving the finest, handcrafted tacos in town. We take immense pride in using only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients sourced locally, ensuring that each bite bursts with authentic Mexican taste.

Our talented team of chefs skillfully combines traditional recipes with modern flair, presenting a diverse menu that caters to every palate. Whether you're a fan of classic carne asada, crave the zesty kick of fish tacos, or prefer plant-based options, we have something special for everyone.
At Taco Shap, we strive for excellence in both taste and service. Our warm and friendly staff is dedicated to creating a memorable dining experience for you and your loved ones. We're not just a restaurant; we're a place where memories are made, and friendships are forged.

So come on over and savor the flavors of Mexico at Taco Shap. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite during lunch or celebrating a special occasion, our tacos will leave you wanting more. Thank you for being a part of our journey, and we look forward to serving you the best tacos in town!
