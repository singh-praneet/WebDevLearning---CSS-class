Welcome to "My Little Taco Shop"

Your ultimate destination for mouthwatering tacos that will transport your taste buds to a realm of pure.

At our cozy and vibrant taco shop, we take pride in crafting each taco with love and creativity, using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients. Our diverse menu features a tantalizing array of flavors, from classic carne asada and succulent al pastor to zesty shrimp ceviche and hearty vegetarian options, ensuring there's a perfect taco for every palate.

At "Taco Heaven," we embrace the spirit of community and celebration. Our friendly staff and vibrant atmosphere create a warm and inviting space for friends and families to come together and enjoy a truly authentic culinary journey.
Come join us, and let our tacos speak for themselves a burst of flavors that will keep you coming back for more. Viva los tacos!

Our Tacos - A Fiesta for Your Palate

  • Classic Carnitas - Succulent pork slow-cooked to perfection, topped with tangy salsa, diced onions, and cilantro.
  • Savory Carne Asada - Grilled, tender steak seasoned with our secret blend of spices, garnished with fresh guacamole and pico de gallo.
  • Zesty Chicken Tinga - Shredded chicken simmered in a smoky chipotle sauce, adorned with avocado slices and lime wedges.
  • Mouthwatering Veggie Delight - A medley of grilled vegetables, black beans, queso fresco, and our special salsa, a vegetarian's dream.
  • Seafood Sensation - Enjoy the taste of the ocean with our shrimp or fish tacos, drizzled with our signature creamy chipotle sauce.

Sides and More

  • Guacamole and Chips - Creamy avocado goodness served with crispy tortilla chips.
  • Queso Fundido - Melted cheese with chorizo, mushrooms, or poblano peppers, served with warm tortillas.
  • Mexican Street Corn - Grilled corn on the cob smothered in mayo, cotija cheese, and chili powder.
  • Refreshing Agua Frescas - Quench your thirst with our variety of flavored aguas frescas.
